What AI Extracts from CAD
  • Part geometry: envelope dimensions, volume, surface area, wall thickness
  • Feature recognition: holes, pockets, ribs, undercuts, threads, datum features
  • Material inference: visual texture, density estimation, typical grade selection
  • Process selection: AI matches geometry to most cost-effective manufacturing route
  • Cycle time estimation: material removal rate, tool path length, fixture strategy
  • Complexity classification: simple / moderate / complex — drives machine selection and OEE
  • DFM flags: thin walls, tight radii, undercuts, critical tolerances — flagged before costing

Engineering Depth
Should-Cost Model Architecture
Technical Depth
Industry-Grade Cost Models — Not Spreadsheet Estimates
Built on aPriori-calibrated benchmarks, IPC standards, and first-principles engineering.
📦 Material Database
Alloy steels, aluminium grades, engineering plastics, elastomers, composites, PCB laminates, copper — all with live density, price and grade logic. FX-adjusted per region.
⚙️ Machine & Rate Library
Rate library with 50+ machine types — CNC centres, presses, moulding machines, SMT lines, reflow ovens, test equipment. Rates editable per project/region.
🛤️ Routing & Process Logic
AI-driven operation sequencing. Each operation has: machine ID, cycle time (hr), OEE, setup time, labour grade, and overhead allocation.
🔩 Tooling & NRE Amortisation
Dies, moulds, fixtures, PCB NRE — all properly amortised over annual volume. Tooling life curves for press tools and EDM electrodes.
📉 Yield & Scrap Models
First-pass yield computed from 10 penalty factors per commodity: layer count, trace width, via type, HDI structure, impedance control, BGA pitch, alloy grade, section thickness and more.
🌍 Regional Cost Multipliers
20 manufacturing regions — each with calibrated labour tiers (skilled / semi-skilled / engineer / inspector), energy costs, and machine hour rates. Currency FX auto-corrects.
📊 Benchmarks & Sensitivity
Every cost element benchmarked against aPriori/Cleansheet industry ranges. Tornado chart shows top 10 cost drivers at ±10%. Scenario A/B/C comparison.
🤖 AI-Driven Assumptions
Where geometry is unknown, Claude AI fills assumptions using engineering judgement — material, complexity, process, geometry — with transparent explanation of every assumption made.
