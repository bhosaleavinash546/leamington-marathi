About Us

Leamington Marathi is a social and cultural group based in Leamington Spa / Warwick, West Midlands. We are families from Maharashtra, India who have made the UK our home — and together we have built a true “home away from home”.

महाराष्ट्रातून इंग्लंडमध्ये आलेल्या आम्ही सर्व मराठी कुटुंबांनी मिळून हा परिवार उभा केला आहे. सण-उत्सव, संगीत, कला आणि एकमेकांची साथ — यातून आम्ही आपल्या मातीशी असलेलं नातं जपतो.

The group comprises over 400+ families and continues to grow steadily, serving as a hub for Marathi families in the Midlands region. Our mission is to preserve and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Maharashtra while fostering a sense of community and belonging among our members.

We are a non-profit community organisation, run entirely by volunteers, and we are working towards becoming a registered CIO (Charitable Incorporated Organisation).

आमचे कार्यक्रम

Our Annual Events

Marathi families unite throughout the year to celebrate our culture.

January | जानेवारी

Haldi Kunku – हळदी-कुंकू

A traditional gathering for Marathi ladies to celebrate Makar Sankranti — tilgul, saris and sisterhood. तिळगूळ घ्या, गोड गोड बोला!

70+ participants
April | एप्रिल

Gudhi Padwa – गुढीपाडवा शुभेच्छा समारोह

Welcoming the Maharashtrian New Year with the traditional gudhi, music and festivities.

200+ participants
May | मे

Shivrajyotsav – शिवराज्योत्सव

Celebrating Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti and Maharashtra Din with skits, music, dance and art performances. जय भवानी, जय शिवाजी!

280+ participants
September | सप्टेंबर

Ganeshotsav – गणेशोत्सव

At the Shree Krishna Mandir Ganesh Festival we prepare and serve authentic Maharashtrian food, and our Dhol Tasha Pathak leads the Ganesh procession. गणपती बाप्पा मोरया!

700+ participants
Oct / Nov | दिवाळी

Diwali Celebration – दिवाळी भेट

The festival of lights with cultural performances and faraal, plus our Dhol group performing at the town's Diwali Lights Switch-On. दिवाळीच्या हार्दिक शुभेच्छा!

280+ & 700+ participants

Leamington Marathi
Dhol Tasha Pathak 🥁 ढोल-ताशा पथक

Our proudest initiative — a traditional Maharashtrian Dhol Tasha troupe that brings the thunder of Pune's streets to the heart of England. The Pathak performs at the Ganesh procession, the town's Diwali Lights Switch-On and community events across the region.

ढोल-ताशाचा गजर झाला की लेमिंगटनच्या रस्त्यांवरही महाराष्ट्र अवतरतो!
