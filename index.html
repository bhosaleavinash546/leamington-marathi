About Us

Leamington Marathi is a social and cultural group based in Leamington Spa / Warwick, West Midlands. We are families from Maharashtra, India who have made the UK our home — and together we have built a true “home away from home”.

महाराष्ट्रातून इंग्लंडमध्ये आलेल्या आम्ही सर्व मराठी कुटुंबांनी मिळून हा परिवार उभा केला आहे. सण-उत्सव, संगीत, कला आणि एकमेकांची साथ — यातून आम्ही आपल्या मातीशी असलेलं नातं जपतो.

The group comprises over 400+ families and continues to grow steadily, serving as a hub for Marathi families in the Midlands region. Our mission is to preserve and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Maharashtra while fostering a sense of community and belonging among our members.

We are a non-profit community organisation, run entirely by volunteers, and we are working towards becoming a registered CIO (Charitable Incorporated Organisation).

आमची वाटचाल

Our Journey

From a handful of families to a thriving community.

The Beginning

A few families, one WhatsApp group – सुरुवात

Marathi families in Leamington Spa and Warwick find each other and start meeting — the seed of a community, planted over chaha and conversation.

2023

First big celebrations – पहिले मोठे सण

Ganeshotsav at Shree Krishna Mandir and our first Diwali Bhet bring hundreds together — proof that Leamington was ready for a Marathi mandal of its own.

2024

The Dhol Tasha Pathak is born – ढोल-ताशा पथक

Our proudest initiative takes shape: drums arrive from India, practice sessions begin, and the Pathak's first public performances turn heads across town.

2025

400+ families strong – ४००+ कुटुंबे

Shivrajyotsav draws 280+ people, the Pathak leads the town's Diwali Lights Switch-On before 700+, and the group becomes the hub for Marathi families across the Midlands.

6 June 2026

World Book of Records – विश्वविक्रम!

Our Dhol Tasha Pathak performs in London alongside 13 pathaks and 270 players — a historic Maharashtrian gathering etched into the World Book of Records. मराठी संस्कृतीच्या जागतिक वैभवाचा ऐतिहासिक उत्सव!

🏆 13 pathaks · 270 players
Today

Onwards! – पुढची झेप

Our official website is live, sponsors are on board, and we're working towards registered CIO charity status. जय महाराष्ट्र! 🚩

Dhol Tasha Pathak 🥁 ढोल-ताशा पथक
