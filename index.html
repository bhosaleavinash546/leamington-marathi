About Us

Leamington Marathi is a vibrant social and cultural group based in Leamington Spa/Warwick, West Midlands. Established to bring together Marathi families, our mission is to celebrate and preserve our rich cultural heritage while creating a home away from home.

Through festivals, musical performances, cultural programs, and community gatherings, we recreate the spirit of Maharashtra in the UK and strengthen the social bonds among Indian families.
