Negotiation Insights & Cost Levers

Key Cost Drivers

Magnet material volatility — Neodymium prices swing ±20% with rare earth supply chains
Voice coil copper pricing — tracked against LME copper spot, ±10% annual variance
Labour-intensive final assembly — accounts for 12–18% of process cost in high-wage regions
DSP calibration overhead — per-unit testing adds fixed cost regardless of volume

Negotiation Levers

Switch to ferrite magnet on low-end models — saves 15–25% on material cost
Increase coil-winding automation — reduces labour exposure by 8–12% per unit
Localise cone manufacturing to the assembly country — cuts cross-border logistics
Consolidate to 2–3 strategic suppliers for volume pricing (5–10% discount potential)
